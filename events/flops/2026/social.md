## Excursion and Banquet

The FLOPS 2026 excursion and banquet are
held on Day 2 (Wednesday May 27).

All FLOPS 2026 attendees are welcome to
participate in the excursions.
The banquet is open to
invited speakers, regular participants, and
participants who purchased banquet tickets.

## IMPORTANT NOTICE

All participants in the excursion MUST refrain from
eating NATTO or yogurt from the morning of May 26,
the day before the excursion.
This is a strict requirement. If you have
NATTO or yogurt on May 26 or 27,
you will NOT be allowed to participate in
Excursion 2, although
you may still join Excursion 1 and Banquet.

This requirement is necessary
because the SAKE brewery is highly sensitive to
contamination.
Japanese SAKE production uses koji mold,
which can be affected by
NATTO bacteria and yogurt cultures.

## Excursion 1: Yanaka-no-Mori

Yanaka-no-Mori (formerly the residence of Mr. Yanaka)
is a traditional Japanese building constructed
by Ryujiro Yanaka during the SHOWA era (around 1938-1953).
It is located in the Hojo district
at the foot of Mt. Tsukuba.
The building has been designated 
a National Important Cultural Property
by the Japanese government.

The Japanese web page is available 
[here](https://www.yanakanomori.org).

## Excursion 2: Urazato Shuzo 

Urazato Shuzo is a SAKE brewery,
which is famous for the SAKE brand KIRI TSUKUBA.

Participants can tour large KURA
(traditional SAKE storage), and
learn about the SAKE brewing process.
A small SAKE tasting will also be available.

## Banquet: Tenryu-Honten 

The banquet is held at Tenryu-Honten,
a restaurant serving a traditional Japanese cuisine.
