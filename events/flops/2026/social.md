## Excursion and Banquet

The FLOPS 2026 excursion and banquet are
held on Day 2 (Wednesday May 27).

All attendees of FLOPS 2026 can attend excursions.
Invited/regular participants and
those who bought banquet tickets can attend banquets.

## IMPORTANT NOTICE

All participants of the excursion MUST refrain from
eating NATTO or Yogurt from the day before the excursion,
i.e., from the morning of May 26.
This is a strict rule; if you happen to have had
NATTO or Yogurt on May 26 or 27,
you cannot attend Excursion 2 (but can attend
Excursion 1 and Banquet).

The rule is due to the sensitivity of the SAKE brewery;
a Japanese SAKE makes use of 'koji' mold,
which is very sensitive to contamination from
NATTO bacteria and Yogurt cultures.

## Excursion 1: Yanaka-no-Mori

Yanaka-no-Mori (formerly, Mr. Yanaka's house)
is a traditional Japanese architecture built
by Ryujiro Yanaka in the SHOWA era (around 1938-1953).
It is located in the Hojo district
on the foot of Mt. Tsukuba.
The architecture is designated as
a National Important Cultural Property
by the Japanese government.

The web page in Japanese is available 
[here](https://yananomori.org).

## Excursion 2: Urazato Shuzo 

Urazato Shuzo is a SAKE brewery,
which is famous of the SAKE brand "KIRI TSUKUBA".

We can watch large "KURA" to store SAKE, and
listen to how they make SAKE.
We can also taste (a small quantity of) SAKE there.

## Banquet: Tenryu-Honten 

The Banquet is held in the Tenryu-Honten restaurant,
which serves a traditional Japanese style dinner.
