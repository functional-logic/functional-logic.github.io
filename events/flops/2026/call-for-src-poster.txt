Subject: FLOPS 2026 Student Research Competition and Posters

======================================================================

		        *** FLOPS 2026 ***
   18th International Symposium on Functional and Logic Programming
	   *** Student Research Competition and Posters ***
	               Call for Submissions

		    May 26-28, 2026, Akita, Japan

	   https://functional-logic.org/events/flops/2026/

======================================================================

FLOPS 2026 Student Research Competition (SRC) provides opportunities
for students to present their work and receive feedback from the
community. Prizes will be awarded to students based on the judges'
evaluation.

The associated poster session welcomes contributions from any
FLOPS 2026 participants, not necessarily students.

## Submission to SRC

Submissions to SRC must fall within the scope of FLOPS 2026 and
must describe work that has not been published in refereed venues.
The lead author of the submission must be a full-time student.
Submissions that do not satisfy these conditions will be rejected.
If you have questions about the scope, contact the SRC organizer.

To apply for SRC, submit an extended abstract (a PDF file, A4 size,
10pt, max. 3 pages excluding bibliography) by the submission deadline.
If accepted, submit a poster (a PDF file, A0 size) by the poster
deadline, and present it in the Poster Session of FLOPS 2026.
The award-giving ceremony will be held on the last day of FLOPS 2026.

## Non-SRC Poster Submission 

Non-SRC Poster Submissions must fall within the scope of FLOPS,
which describes the authors' published or unpublished work.

To present your poster at FLOPS 2026, submit a short abstract
(text format) by the submission deadline. If accepted, submit a
poster (a PDF file, A0 size) by the poster deadline, and present
it in the Poster Session of FLOPS 2026.
