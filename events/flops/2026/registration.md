FLOPS 2026 will be held as an in-person event.
Participants must register online (no on-site registration
is possible).
The early registration deadline is April 25, 23:59 JST (UTC+9).

## Registration Fees

| Registration type | Early (until April 25 JST) | Late |
|:-------:|:---------:|:---------:|
| Regular | 50000 JPY | 60000 JPY |
| Student | 20000 JPY | 30000 JPY |

Regular registration includes
attendance to the symposium, coffee and tea, excursion, and banquet.
Student registration includes 
attendance to the symposium, coffee and tea, and excursion, but not banquet.
The student registration is applicable for full-time students.

Note that at least one author for each accepted paper must register as regular registration.

## Registration Site

Registration site will be open by the end of February, 2026.
