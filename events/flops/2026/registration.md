FLOPS 2026 will be held physically (in person).
Participants must register online before the meeting (no on-site registration is possible).
The early registration deadline is April 25, 23:59 JST (UTC+9).

## Registration Fees

| Registration type | Early (until April 25 JST) | Late |
|:-------:|:---------:|:---------:|
| Regular | 50000 JPY | 60000 JPY |
| Student | 20000 JPY | 30000 JPY |
| Extra banquet | 10000 JPY | 10000 JPY |

Regular registration includes coffee, excursion, and banquet.
Student registration includes coffee and excursion, but not banquet.
The student registration is applicable to full-time students.

Note that at least one author for each accepted paper must register as regular registration.

## Registration Site

The registration site will be open by the end of February 2026.
