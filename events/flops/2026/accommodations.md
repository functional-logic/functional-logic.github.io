Tsukuba has many affordable hotels (the fees are much less than the hotels in Tokyo).
Participants of FLOPS 2026 are encouraged to book hotels by themselves. 

Notice: the local organizers have booked a limited number of single rooms in the university's guesthouse
from May 25 till May 28 (four nights).
The guesthouse offers single rooms with very reasonable price (5500 JPY per night).
Contact the organizer <span style="color=red">flops.tsukuba@gmail.com</span> for details.

## Recommended Hotels near Tsukuba Center

Below you can find a list of hotels that are within walking distance of Tsukuba Center.
We classify hotels by class (fee), style (western or traditional Japanese), and distance from Tsukuba Center:
"very close" means "< 5 minutes" on foot, "close" means "< 15 minutes", and "modest" means "< 30 minutes".

| Class       | Style | Distance | Hotel |
|:-----------:|:-----------------------------------------:|:--:|:--:|
| High        | Japanese | Close | [Hotel Grand Shinonome](https://www.hg-shinonome.co.jp/english/) | 
| High        | Western | Very close | [Daiwa Roynet Hotel Tsukuba](https://www.daiwaroynet.jp/en/tsukuba/) |
| Middle      | Western | Very close | [Hotel Nikko Tsukuba](https://www.okura-nikko.com/japan/tsukuba/hotel-nikko-tsukuba/)| 
| Middle      | Western | Close  | [Hotel JAL City Tsukuba](https://www.tsukuba.hoteljalcity.com/eng/)| 
| Reasonable  | Western | Modest | [Hotel Route Tsukuba](https://www.route-tsukuba.jp/en/) |
