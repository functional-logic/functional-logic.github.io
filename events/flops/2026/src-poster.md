## Student Research Competition (SRC) and Posters

FLOPS 2026 will have the Student Research Competition (SRC),
which aims to provide opportunities for students
to present their work and receive feedback from the community.
Prizes will be given to the students
based on the evaluation by the judges.

The associated poster session welcomes
contributions by any participants of FLOPS 2026
(not necessarily from the participants of SRC).

## Submission to SRC

Submissions to SRC
must fall within the scope of FLOPS, and
must describe the author's unpublished work.
The lead author of the submission must be a full-time student.
Submissions that do not satisfy these conditions will be rejected.
If you have questions about the scope,
contact the organizer of SRC.

To participate in the SRC,
submit (1) an extended abstract (max. 3 pages, excluding bibliography) by the submission deadline,
and (2) a poster (PDF file) by the poster deadline.

The student author of each poster must attend FLOPS 2026
and give a poster presentation.
The award-giving ceremony
will be held on the last day of FLOPS 2026.

## Submission to non-SRC posters

Submissions to the posters (but not to SRC)
must fall within the scope of FLOPS, which
describes the authors' published or unpublished work.
Submissions that do not satisfy these conditions will be rejected.
